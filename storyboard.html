Storyboard

This storyboard page outlines the structure, layout, and overall design of my website. It presents wireframes, a site plan, and the visual theme used throughout the pages, providing a clear overview of how the website is organized and how users will navigate through its content. This section serves as a blueprint that connects design choices with functionality and user experience.

Site Plan

    Index Page

    Provides a brief introduction and a navigation bar to all other pages.

    Professional Page

    Mirrors my Resume, showcasing my career goals, vision, mission, and skills

    Personality Page

    Highlights my strongest traits, hobbies, and personality assessment results.

    Personal Development Page

    Storyboard Page

Theme of the site

    Color scheme: Light grey and dark grey with red accents.

    Font style: Arial, easy to read.

Features I added to the website

  • Navigation Bar: Available on all pages for seamless nagivation across website.
  • Internal Bookmark Link: At least one internal link (bookmark) to enhance the navigation experience within a page.
  • Personality Test: Embedded screenshot of the personality test results along with a reflective analysis.
  • Free Hosting: The website will be hosted on a free platform and a link will be provided here.
